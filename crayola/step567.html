• Using the glue gun, quickly draw a line down each of the crayons and place them on the canvas. If you are keeping the Crayola lables attached, you can use the seal of the wrapper as a guide. Trace the seal for a straight line of glue. Be careful because the glue will be hot!
  • Line your floor and surrounding walls with newspapers. The crayons will splatter so it might get messy! To avoid heavy cleanup, try to do this project outside instead.
  • Place your canvas against a wall at an angle so that the melted crayon will drip downward.
