Example Shopping Scenarios

Walgreens - April 15th 2012

Sometimes Walgreens can seem like a complicated place to coupon shop, but don't get discouraged. Once you get the hang of it, Walgreens can actually be a lot of fun. Not only can you used Manufacturer coupons here, but you can stack them with In-Store coupons to maximize your savings. Walgreens also has a promotional Register Rewards program, which allows you to use catalinas on future purchases. Here is an example of a shopping scenario that takes advantage of all three, by rolling rewards (RRs) between products! Breakdown:

TOTAL PAID: $25.17, Retail Value: $130. An 81% Savings!

Transaction #1:
1. Get $9.00 RR WYB Nasal Ease Allergy Relief Spray .18oz, $9.00
USED $5/1 Nasal Ease Allergy Reliever (RP 03/11/12)
2. Get $.99 RR WYB Pepsi Next 20oz, $.99
3. Kraft Macaroni & Cheese BOGO FREE @ $1.79 (With In-Ad Coupon)
USED In-Ad Coupon
USED Macsurance Facebook Giveaway (FREE Up to $1.60)
4. Get $6.00 RR WYB Similasan Allergy Relief .68oz, $6.00
USED $2 Peelie on the package
PAID: $9.18, Got $.99 RR and $9.00 RR and $6.00 RR
