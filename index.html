about

I have been involved with web and graphic design in the professional industry for 4+ years. In 2013, I obtained a BFA in Web Design and Interactive Media at the Illinois Institute of Art - Schaumburg.

I focus on responsive, attractive and coherent designs. It is important to combine functionality and aesthetics to create an effective website. I enjoy coding, design, writing/hierarchy, typography and other nerdy stuff. I see myself in a future front-end development position. However, I am eager to learn all aspects of creative technologies.

INSPIRATION: My logo, as well as this layout design, was inspired by a my recent spark of interest sacred geometry and the flower of life. I hope you enjoy my online portfolio!

SKILLS/INTERESTS: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript/JQuery, Responsive Design, PHP, Wordpress, Bootstrap, Graphic Design, Research and Development, Reading and Writing, and Cooking
